Family Movie Night

Time: 6:30 – 8:30 PM
Location: School Gymnasium
Date: Friday, March 21, 2026

Join us for a fun evening at the school gym! We'll be screening a family-friendly movie on the big screen with popcorn, snacks, and drinks available for purchase.

Bring your own blankets and chairs to get comfortable. Doors open at 6:00 PM and the movie starts at 6:30 PM sharp. All Westmont families are welcome — siblings and extended family encouraged!

Snack proceeds go directly toward funding the end-of-year field day for all students. We need volunteers to help with setup, concessions, and cleanup.
